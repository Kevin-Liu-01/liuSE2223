Procedure

How the experiment was carried out


  1. Step 1

    Prepare C. elegans

    • Prepare starter c elegans plate
    • Heat and pour NGM
    • Begin seeding E. Coli

  2. Step 2

    Prepare Dilutions

    • Prepare Permachrome Ink
    • Make 0.1%, 1%, 10%, 100% dilutions of ink
    • Apply to C. Elegans plates

  3. Step 3

    Chunk C. Elegans

    • One 60 mm plate was seeded with N2 Wild-Type Caenorhabditis elegans to be used to chunk additional plates
    • Allow C. elegans to proliferate on this initial plate
    • C. elegans from the plate were chunked onto the other six test case plates

  4. Step 4

    Data Collection

    • Frequently track C. elegans populations for each dilution plate
    • Record notes and observations
    • 1-2 months of data collection

  5. Step 5

    Data Analysis

    • T-Test taken for ethanol control vs. control to determine they were not significantly different
    • Further T-Tests comparing populations of treated populations to ethanol control
    • P-values generated by comparing groups
    • Plates with lowest p-values indicated most probable significance of ink
